Account Deletion Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

This Account Deletion Policy explains how you can request deletion of your Hypertroph account and what happens to your data when you do. This policy applies to all users of the App, including clients and administrators.

By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Account Deletion Policy. If you have any questions, please contact us using the information in Section 13.

2. Your Right to Delete Your Account

You have the right to request deletion of your account under laws such as GDPR and CCPA. We process requests in accordance with these regulations.

3. How to Request Account Deletion

3.1 For Client Accounts

  • Contact Support: Email siddharth@hypertroph.com
  • Through Your Administrator: Ask your administrator to process deletion.

3.2 For Administrator Accounts

Administrators must verify identity and transfer clients before deletion.

4. What Data Gets Deleted

All personal, health, fitness, nutrition, and training data associated with your account will be deleted permanently.

5. Data That May Be Retained

  • Financial records and legal data (for compliance)
  • Anonymized analytics and aggregated data
  • Backup data (for up to 90 days)
  • Data stored by third-party services (OpenAI, USDA, HealthKit)

6. Deletion Process and Timeline

  1. Verification: We verify your identity.
  2. Deactivation: Account access is disabled.
  3. Deletion: Data is deleted within 7 business days; backups within 90 days.

7. Irreversibility of Account Deletion

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. You cannot recover your account or data once deleted.

8. Data Export Before Deletion

You may request data export before deletion by emailing siddharth@hypertroph.com. Exports are processed within 14 business days.

9. Special Considerations

  • Clients under administrators will notify their admin.
  • Admins must transfer clients before deletion.
  • Active subscriptions are cancelled during deletion.

10. Security and Verification

We require password or email verification before deletion. Fraud prevention checks may apply.

11. Appeals and Disputes

If your account was deleted in error, email siddharth@hypertroph.com within 14 days. Limited recovery may be possible if within 90 days.

12. Children's Accounts

Parents can request deletion of a child’s account with verification.

13. Contact Information

Email: siddharth@hypertroph.com
Subject: “Account Deletion Request” or “Account Deletion Policy Inquiry”
App: Hypertroph

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be announced via app notification or email.

15. Compliance and Legal Basis

This policy complies with GDPR, CCPA, and other data protection laws.

16. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take to delete my account?
A: Primary deletion: 7 business days. Complete deletion: 90 days.

Q: Can I recover my account?
A: No, deletion is permanent.

Q: Will my subscription be cancelled?
A: Yes, subscriptions are cancelled during deletion.

Q: What about my photos?
A: All stored photos are permanently deleted.
